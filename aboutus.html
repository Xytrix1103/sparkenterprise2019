A Message From Us

Hello from all the Achievers of Spark Enterprise!

Hi there! Would you like to know us better? If yes - read on!


We chose the name "Spark" for several reasons - one, because we aspire to reach out to our customers with our products that aim to promote the STEAM concept. We also hope to incorporate electrical elements into our products! Oh, and thirdly we hope Spark Enterprise lives up to its name - and "spark"s up your life too!


Our tagline - Igniting the Future - really means we're about to light the way ahead with our bright product ideas! Stay tuned to see how!


Seen our logo? Does it look like a spark to you? Yep, that's because it is! The yellow and orange colors, as well as the spark-like design, are meant to symbolise electricity and the STEAM movement.


We do our best to come up with unconventional product ideas, making them as unique and fresh as possible - so to stand out from the rest. It is our sincere hope that by doing this, our products are able to satisfy our customers' needs - while being aesthetically pleasing too!


It is our principle that we maintain our best performance when faced with customers. Politeness and courtesy - not forgetting friendliness - are all very important during the YE Sales Fair! We hope you'll be there to support us!

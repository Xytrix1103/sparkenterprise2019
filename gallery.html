Recent activites

Our Achievers have many opportunities to expose themselves to new experiences and knowledge. Here are some of our most memorable ones!

Achievers Workshop at DELL

Date: 14th April 2019

An entertaining workshop hosted by our corporate sponsor, DELL. Our hosts were friendly and cheerful, sharing sessions were really fun and interactive, food was great too! All of us at Spark Enterprise are super grateful for the team building activities to foster a close-knit and productive relationship between our Achievers. Lots of joyful laughter and friendly banter could be heard - very loudly! We tookk many funny photos too!

JA International Trade Challenge

Date: 24th April 2019

An educational experience for us all. Through this event, our Achievers realised the importance of analytical skills and preparation while preparing for their simulated product pitching session. One of our two teams from Spark Enterprise managed to stand out amidst the strong competition, being the overall winner of the challenge! However, the most important thing is that we gained a lot from participating in the ITC 2019. (Huge shoutout to JA for giving Spark Enterprise this opportunity!)

Corporate Social Responsibilities

Date: 19th June 2019

For Spark Enterprise's CSR project, we decided to take on a unprecedented task - distributing food to the homeless. Our Achievers did everything from packing the food, giving them out, and even the cleaning duties that followed. This activity made us realise that we should all be grateful for the things we have at the moment and not want more. We would also like to sincerely thank Kechara Soup Kitchen for their selfless contributions.
